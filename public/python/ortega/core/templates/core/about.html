Our Story

Building Communities Since 2010

Founded in 2010, Iligan2L Construction Supply started as a small family business with a simple mission: to provide quality construction materials at fair prices to the local community.

Over the years, we've grown from a single small store to becoming one of the most trusted construction supply companies in Iligan City. Our growth has been fueled by our unwavering commitment to quality, reliability, and customer satisfaction.

Today, we continue to serve contractors, builders, and homeowners with the same dedication and passion that has defined our business from day one. We've expanded our product range, improved our services, and built lasting relationships with our customers and suppliers.
