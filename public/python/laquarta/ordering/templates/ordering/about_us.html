About Us

Welcome to Jaimes Lechon Bayug! We are dedicated to providing the best lechon in Iligan City. Our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction has made us a favorite among locals and visitors alike.

Our Mission

To serve delicious, quality food that brings people together and creates memorable experiences.

Our Story

Marc, his grandfather's lechon recipe was originally and solely made for their family until other people tasted Sir Jaime's delightful lechon recipe and requested for him to make one for them. He started as a home-service “mang-leletchon” and later on established Jaime's Lechon Bayug in the 80s.
